Part 1 — Structure (1–15)
Выбери вариант который заполняет пропуск
1/40Structure
The discovery of antibiotics in the early twentieth century ______ medicine forever, saving millions of lives.
✅ B — transformed
«in the early twentieth century» = конкретный период в прошлом → Simple Past.
📌 Конкретный период/дата = Simple Past.
2/40Structure
Not until the 1960s ______ begin to understand the structure of DNA.
✅ B — did scientists
«Not until» в начале = инверсия (вспомогательный глагол перед подлежащим).
📌 Not until / Never / Rarely в начале → инверсия: did/does/has + S.
3/40Structure
The committee recommended ______ the new environmental regulations immediately.
✅ A — implementing
recommend → + gerund (-ing). Verb complement: recommend doing sth.
📌 recommend / suggest / consider / enjoy → + V-ing.
4/40Structure
______ of the proposals submitted by the research team was accepted by the funding committee.
✅ B — None
«None of the proposals» — «none of» + мн.ч. существительное = ни одного из. «Neither» = только для двух вариантов. «No one» = только для людей.
📌 none of + noun (мн.ч.). neither = только для двух. no one = только люди.
5/40Structure
The ancient Romans were remarkable engineers, ______ constructed roads and aqueducts that have survived for thousands of years.
✅ B — who
Антецедент «The ancient Romans» = люди → «who». Придаточное между запятыми → non-restrictive → «who» (не «that»).
📌 Люди + non-restrictive (с запятыми) → who.
6/40Structure
The population of urban areas is growing ______ that many cities are struggling to provide adequate infrastructure.
✅ B — so rapidly
«so + adverb + that» — наречие «rapidly» без существительного → «so». «such» требует noun.
📌 so + adv/adj + that. such + (a) adj + noun + that.
7/40Structure
By the time the expedition reached the summit, three climbers ______ already turned back due to altitude sickness.
✅ B — had
«By the time» + Past Simple → Past Perfect (had + V3) для более раннего действия.
📌 By the time [Past] → [had + V3].
8/40Structure
The professor asked the students to read the assigned chapters, take detailed notes, ______ for a discussion in the next class.
✅ B — to prepare
«asked students to read... to take... to prepare» — параллельные инфинитивы. «ask someone to do» → to-infinitive list.
📌 Параллелизм: to read, to take, to prepare.
9/40Structure
It was not until the nineteenth century ______ scientists began to understand the role of microorganisms in disease.
✅ B — that
«It was not until... that» — фиксированная конструкция с эмфатическим «it».
📌 It was not until [time] THAT [S + V].
10/40Structure
Neither the new policy nor the old regulations ______ adequate protection for endangered species.
✅ B — provide
Neither…nor → согласование с ближайшим: «the old regulations» (мн.ч.) → «provide».
📌 Neither A nor B → глагол согласуется с B.
11/40Structure
The researchers found ______ regular exercise significantly reduces the risk of chronic diseases.
✅ B — that
«find + that-clause»: «found that S + V» — стандартная конструкция с noun clause.
📌 find / show / prove / conclude + that + S + V.
12/40Structure
The Amazon River carries more water than ______ river system in the world.
✅ A — any other
«more than any other» — стандартная превосходная конструкция. «more than all other» — неверно (logical comparison).
📌 X is more [adj] than any other = X is the most [adj].
13/40Structure
______ the earthquake caused massive destruction, the city managed to rebuild within a decade.
✅ B — Although
После пропуска — полное предложение с глаголом «caused». Нужен союз → «Although».
📌 Although + S + V. Despite/In spite of + noun phrase.
14/40Structure
The population of India is larger than ______ any European country.
✅ B — that of
«population» = ед.ч. → «that of» заменяет «the population of» в сравнении.
📌 that of = ед.ч. noun в сравнении. those of = мн.ч.
15/40Structure
Scientists have studied the long-term effects of climate change ______ the early 1970s.
✅ B — since
Present Perfect + «since» + точка в прошлом (the early 1970s). «for» + период продолжительности.
📌 since + точка = Present Perfect. for + длительность = Present Perfect.
P2
Part 2 — Written Expression (16–40)
Нажми на кнопку части где ошибка (A/B/C/D)
16/40Written Expr.
The (A) recently discovery of a new antibiotic (B) has raised hopes for (C) treating drug-resistant (D) infections.
✅ A — «recently» → «recent»
После «The» нужно прилагательное + noun. «recently» — наречие. Нужно «The recent discovery».
📌 The + adjective + noun. recent (adj) не recently (adv).
17/40Written Expr.
Each of the scientists (A) on the research team (B) were required to submit (C) a detailed report of (D) their findings.
✅ B — «were required» → «was required»
«Each» → всегда ед.ч. → «was required».
📌 Each/Every/Either/Neither → ед.ч.
18/40Written Expr.
The new legislation is (A) different (B) than previous environmental laws (C) in several (D) important respects.
✅ B — «than» → «from»
Collocation: «different FROM» (не than).
📌 different FROM · similar TO · responsible FOR.
19/40Written Expr.
(A) Discovering in 1953, the structure of DNA (B) represented one of (C) the most significant (D) achievements in modern science.
✅ A — «Discovering» → «Discovered»
Субъект «the structure» — его открыли (пассив) → «Discovered» (past participle).
📌 Пассивное причастие: субъект = объект действия → V-ed.
20/40Written Expr.
The evidence gathered (A) by the investigation team (B) strongly suggest that the fire (C) was (D) deliberately set.
✅ B — «suggest» → «suggests»
«The evidence» = несчётное = ед.ч. → «suggests».
📌 evidence/information/news = несчётные = ед.ч.
21/40Written Expr.
The number of languages (A) spoken natively around (B) the world (C) are estimated to (D) exceed seven thousand.
✅ C — «are» → «is»
«The number of» → ед.ч. → «is».
📌 The number of = ед.ч. (is). A number of = мн.ч. (are).
22/40Written Expr.
Darwin's (A) theories of evolution are (B) more influential than (C) those of (D) any other biologist.
✅ — Все части верны
Предложение грамматически правильно: theories (мн.) → those of. more influential than those of any other biologist — корректное сравнение.
📌 На тесте такие вопросы редки. Если не видишь ошибки — проверь все части ещё раз внимательно.
23/40Written Expr.
The government's decision (A) to reduce funding for public transportation (B) has resulted (C) on increased traffic (D) congestion.
✅ C — «on» → «in»
Collocation: «result IN» (не on). result in = приводить к.
📌 result IN · result FROM (причина).
24/40Written Expr.
The corporation (A) announced that (B) they had (C) successfully developed a new (D) renewable energy technology.
✅ B — «they» → «it»
«The corporation» = ед.ч. в амер. английском → «it».
📌 company/corporation/government = ед.ч. → it.
25/40Written Expr.
The treaty was (A) signed by both nations (B) after (C) prolonged negotiations (D) had been conclude.
✅ D — «had been conclude» → «had been concluded»
Пассив Past Perfect: had been + V3. «conclude» → «concluded».
📌 Пассив: had been + V3 (past participle).
26/40Written Expr.
The lecture was (A) extreme (B) informative, covering all (C) major aspects of (D) evolutionary biology.
✅ A — «extreme» → «extremely»
Наречие перед прилагательным «informative» → «extremely».
📌 adverb + adjective: extremely informative.
27/40Written Expr.
Both (A) the research and (B) its practical applications (C) has contributed to (D) advances in medical science.
✅ C — «has contributed» → «have contributed»
«Both A and B» → мн.ч. → «have contributed».
📌 Both A and B → мн.ч. (have, are, were).
28/40Written Expr.
The professor explained that (A) the light (B) traveled at approximately 300,000 kilometers (C) per (D) second.
✅ B — «traveled» → «travels»
Скорость света — научный факт → Present Simple (travels), даже если главный глагол в прошедшем.
📌 Научные факты = всегда Present Simple.
29/40Written Expr.
The award (A) was presented to both the director (B) and (C) I at the (D) annual ceremony.
✅ C — «I» → «me»
После «to» (предлог) → объектная форма «me».
📌 После предлога: me/him/her/us/them.
30/40Written Expr.
The new drug was found to be (A) effective, (B) safe, and (C) it costs less than existing treatments in (D) developing countries.
✅ C — «it costs less» → «less expensive»
Параллелизм: effective (adj), safe (adj), [adj]. «it costs less» — не прилагательное.
📌 adj + adj + adj: effective, safe, less expensive.
31/40Written Expr.
An increase (A) of global temperatures (B) has led to (C) more frequent extreme (D) weather events.
✅ A — «of» → «in»
Collocation: «an increase IN» (не of).
📌 increase IN · decrease IN · rise IN.
32/40Written Expr.
(A) Having completed the survey, the results (B) were analyzed by (C) the (D) research team.
✅ A/B — Dangling modifier
«Having completed the survey» — кто? Субъект главного = «the results». Но результаты не проводили опрос. Ошибка в A (структурно): нужно «the research team analyzed» или «Having completed the survey, the research team analyzed the results».
📌 Dangling modifier: субъект оборота ≠ субъект предложения.
33/40Written Expr.
The climate of coastal regions is (A) generally (B) more mild than (C) that of (D) inland areas.
✅ B — «more mild» → «milder»
«mild» — односложное → сравнительная степень «milder» (не «more mild»).
📌 Короткие adj: +er. Длинные: more +. Не оба одновременно!
34/40Written Expr.
Researchers (A) have studied (B) the effects of sleep deprivation (C) since many years and (D) continue to find alarming results.
✅ C — «since» → «for»
«many years» — период продолжительности → «for» (not «since»). since + точка. for + продолжительность.
📌 for + длительность: for many years. since + точка: since 1990.
35/40Written Expr.
The biologist (A) who the committee (B) nominated for the award (C) has conducted groundbreaking (D) genetic research.
✅ A — «who» → «whom»
Объект глагола «nominated» (комитет выдвинул биолога) → объектная форма «whom».
📌 «committee nominated him/her» → him = объект → whom.
36/40Written Expr.
The (A) success of the (B) recently launched satellite (C) has demonstrated that commercial space travel is (D) increasingly viable.
✅ — Нет ошибки (все части верны)
«recently launched» — наречие + причастие = верно. «has demonstrated» = Present Perfect, уместно. Предложение грамматически корректно.
📌 Редко — но иногда все 4 части правильны. Проверяй каждую внимательно.
37/40Written Expr.
(A) Despite the theory faced (B) significant criticism (C) when first proposed, it (D) eventually gained widespread acceptance.
✅ A — «Despite» → «Although»
После «Despite» нужна noun phrase. Здесь полное предложение «the theory faced criticism» → нужен союз «Although».
📌 Although + S + V. Despite + noun phrase.
38/40Written Expr.
The (A) gold is one of (B) the most (C) valued metals throughout (D) human history.
✅ A — «The gold» → «Gold»
«Gold» в общем смысле (металл вообще) → без артикля.
📌 Вещества в общем смысле: ∅ gold, ∅ water, ∅ iron.
39/40Written Expr.
The climate data (A) collected over (B) the past century (C) show a (D) consistent warming trend.
✅ — Нет ошибки
«data» на TOEFL используется как мн.ч. → «show» (не shows). Это правильно. Предложение верно.
📌 data = мн.ч. на TOEFL → show, were, have. (от datum)
40/40Written Expr.
The president (A) announced an increase (B) on funding for (C) scientific (D) research.
✅ B — «on» → «in»
Collocation: «an increase IN» (не on).
📌 increase IN · decrease IN · rise IN.
